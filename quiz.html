This game is a quick and fun music related quiz!


  • The quiz will shuffle twenty music related questions. Underneath the questions you will have
    three options to choose from. Only one of the options is the right one 
  • The correct answer will appear in green and the wrong answer will appear in red.
  • All your right answers is counted in the correct answer box and your wrong answers will be counted and shown in incorrect answer box.
  • At the end of the quiz you can either restart the quiz or return to the home page.

To close this window just click the X in the top of the right corner.
